Glass of Water Recipe

It's so refreshing

I came home today, hot and tired and sweaty. I really needed something cold to drink .I LOVE COLA. The only thing that was cold was water. I never new how good it could be when your'e thirsy.

Ingredients

  • 1 glass
  • ice cold water
  • 2 cubes of ice

Steps

  1. Listen to the tinkle of the ice as you slide it slowly into the glass
  2. Pour water over ice cubes
  3. Lift glass slowly and close your eyes.
  4. Bring glass towards lips
  5. Take a sip and lick lips.
  6. Contemplate akiss.
  7. Bring glass to lips again and drink the rest of the water
  8. Thank God that you are alive
